About Us

We are a mobile app that connects people and merchants in innovative and exciting ways. Based in the Rochester, N.Y. area, we are a group of software engineers and entrepreneurs who want to give both shoppers and merchants more control over their advertising experiences, and their wallets.

We have developed a pay-for-performance model for the 21st Century.

Our behind-the-scenes deal and coupon aggregation technology scours the Internet and pulls together deals, offers and coupons from hundreds of trusted sites like GroupOn, LivingSocial and Amazon Local.

But when you buy a deal through us, we share our revenue with you, putting cash in your pocket.

You can make money and save money in restau- rants, spas, retail stores, and recreation centers, no matter what city you’re in. It's simple to find the deals you want, when you want them. You save money while you make money, and you’re in control.
